Introduction

Every day, there are millions of individuals who interact with HTML on the daily. In fact, these individuals interact with HTML for long periods throughout the day. If you are reading this paragraph, then you too are interacting with HTML. But what is HTML? How is it used in everyday life and how is it applied? The intention of this document is to answer these questions, as well as provide tips for developers who are looking into writing HTML themselves.
